Predicted individual's obesity status using random forest model on dataset of 29 numerical and categorical predictors. We simplified our final model with 10 predictors, resulting in full accuracy and low misclassification rate (0.017). Our significant predictors were height, age, and daily water intake for obesity. This project demonstrates the potential of predictive modeling in addressing global health challenges related to obesity and chronic diseases.
